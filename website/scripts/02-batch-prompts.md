## 🖥️ DEMO (0:30–2:00)

### Step 1: Choose Your Mode

> "Open AutoFlow's side panel. You'll see four creation modes:"
>
> - **Text-to-Video** — Pure text prompts, no images needed
> - **Image-to-Video** — Each prompt gets a reference image
> - **Frame-to-Video** — Chain frames for consistent characters
> - **Ingredients** — Combine multiple elements
>
> "Select the mode that matches your workflow."

**[SCREEN: Show the 4 mode buttons, click Text-to-Video]**

### Step 2: Paste Your Prompts

> "Now paste your prompts into the editor. The rule is simple: **separate each prompt with a blank line.** Each block becomes one task."
>
> "So if I paste my 5-scene Floki animation script…"

**[SCREEN: Paste multi-scene script into the editor]**

> "I click 'Parse Prompts' and boom — 5 prompts, each one labeled with the scene number."

**[SCREEN: Click Parse Prompts → show "5 prompts" badge]**

### Step 3: Review & Queue

> "Scroll down and you'll see each prompt listed with its number. You can review them, add images if needed, then hit 'Add to Queue'."

**[SCREEN: Show prompt list → Add to Queue → queue card appears]**

### Pro Tips

> "A few tips:"
>
> - "Long prompts work great — AutoFlow handles prompts up to 1000+ characters"
> - "Scene numbers are auto-detected from your text"
> - "You can edit individual prompts before queuing"
