## 🖥️ DEMO (0:30–2:15)

### Creating a Queue

> "After parsing your prompts, click 'Add to Queue'. This creates a queue card — think of it as a job ticket for your video batch."

**[SCREEN: Prompts → Add to Queue → queue card appears]**

### Queue Settings Grid

> "Your queue card shows a complete settings grid, split into four sections:"

> **GENERATION**
>
> - **Media** — Video or Image
> - **Type** — Text-to-Video, Image-to-Video, Frame-to-Video, or Ingredients
> - **Model** — Veo 3.1 Fast, Veo 3, or others as they release
> - **Orientation** — Landscape, Portrait, or Square
> - **Generations** — How many videos per prompt (×1, ×2, ×3)

**[SCREEN: Highlight each setting in the GENERATION box]**

> **TIMING**
>
> - **Wait** — How many seconds to wait between prompts (10–20s default)
> - **Typing Mode** — Simulates human typing speed. Useful to avoid detection

**[SCREEN: Show TIMING settings]**

> **DOWNLOAD**
>
> - **Auto-DL** — Automatically download videos when they finish
> - **Quality** — 720p (original), 1080p, or 4K upscaled

**[SCREEN: Show DOWNLOAD settings]**

> **BEHAVIOR**
>
> - **Stop on error** — Halt the queue if a prompt fails, or keep going
> - **Language** — Match your Flow interface language
> - **Target** — Run on the current project or create a new one

**[SCREEN: Show BEHAVIOR settings]**

### Multiple Queues

> "You can create multiple queues with different settings. Maybe queue 1 uses Veo 3.1 Fast for drafts, and queue 2 uses Veo 3 for final renders. They run in order."

**[SCREEN: Show 2 queue cards stacked]**

### Progress Tracking

> "At the bottom of each card you see live stats: total prompts, done, failed, and pending. When you hit Run, these update in real time."

**[SCREEN: Show 4 PROMPTS / 0 DONE / 0 FAILED / 4 PENDING]**
