## 🖥️ DEMO (0:35–2:45)

### Scanning

> "Switch to the Library tab and click 'Scan Project'. AutoFlow scrolls through the entire page, finds every generated tile, and reads the prompt label for each one."

**[SCREEN: Click Library tab → Click Scan Project → "Scanning project..." toast]**

> "When it's done, you get a summary: '5 prompts · 5 assets (5 videos, 0 images)'"

**[SCREEN: Show the stats bar after scan]**

### Grouped Results

> "Your videos are now organized by prompt. Each group shows:"
>
> - The prompt number (oldest = 1, newest = highest)
> - The prompt text as the group title
> - How many videos are in the group
> - A thumbnail for each video

**[SCREEN: Show grouped results — cats: 1 video, sniper: 1 video, etc.]**

> "This is way better than scrolling through Google Flow's grid trying to remember which video belongs to which prompt."

### Filtering

> "Use the filter buttons to show All assets, Photos only, or Videos only. Or use the search bar to find a specific prompt."

**[SCREEN: Click Videos → show filtered → type in search → refined results]**

### Selecting

> "Click the checkbox on any video to select it. Or use 'Select All' to grab everything. The counter updates: 'Selected: 3'."

**[SCREEN: Click checkboxes → Select All → show counter]**

### Batch Download

> "Hit the Download button. Choose your resolution:"
>
> - **720p** — Original quality, instant download
> - **1080p** — Upscaled, slightly slower
> - **4K** — Maximum quality, uses credits
>
> "AutoFlow downloads each video with a clean filename: `queue-name_prompt-001_gen-01.mp4`"

**[SCREEN: Click Download → resolution picker → downloads start]**

### Upscale Without Downloading

> "New feature: you can also trigger upscaling WITHOUT downloading. Click 'Upscale', choose your resolution, and AutoFlow sends each video to Flow's upscaling pipeline. Come back later to download the high-res versions."

**[SCREEN: Click Upscale → select 1080p → upscaling triggered]**

### Retry Failed

> "See a failed tile? Click the Retry button on that specific asset. AutoFlow locates the tile on the page and triggers a re-generation — either using Flow's native Retry or by reusing the prompt."

**[SCREEN: Show retry button on a tile → click → retry triggered]**
