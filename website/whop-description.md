## About (long description)

### What is AutoFlow?

AutoFlow is a Chrome extension that turns Google Flow into a fully automated AI video production line. Instead of typing one prompt at a time and waiting, you paste hundreds of prompts, hit run, and walk away. AutoFlow types them for you, manages the queue, retries failures, and downloads everything when it's done.

### ⚡ Key Features

**Batch Prompt Processing** — Paste 10 or 1,000 prompts. AutoFlow parses them, creates your queue, and runs them one by one — hands-free.

**Smart Queue Management** — Create multiple queues with different settings (model, aspect ratio, resolution). Run them independently. Pause, skip, or retry individual prompts.

**Live Run Monitor** — Watch progress in real-time. See which prompt is generating, which finished, and which failed. Pause and resume anytime.

**Library Scanner** — Scan your Google Flow library and batch download all your existing videos. Group by date, filter by status, download selectively or in bulk.

**Reference Image Mapping** — Attach images per prompt or share them across all prompts. AutoFlow auto-matches character names to your image library.

**Auto-Retry** — If a generation fails (it happens), AutoFlow automatically retries without losing your place in the queue.

**Bulk Download** — Download all results at once in 720p, 1080p, or 4K. No more right-clicking one video at a time.

### 🔧 How It Works

1. Install the AutoFlow Chrome extension
2. Open Google Flow in your browser
3. Open AutoFlow from the side panel
4. Paste your prompts and add images (optional)
5. Click "Add to Queue" → "Run Queue"
6. AutoFlow handles the rest automatically
